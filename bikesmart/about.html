Program

Street Smarts works with middle school staff to arrange the Bike Smart assembly. Students are taught proper helmet fit by local Flatland BMX star Pete Brandt. Free helmets are provided to students who ride bikes, skateboards, and scooters to school*.

The program visits approximately 15 middle schools a year.

* Helmets provided while supplies last.
