Heads Up! Wear a Helmet

Heads Up! is an in-school Traffic Safety Program for young pedestrians and cyclists and drivers in Antioch, Bay Point, Brentwood, Byron, Clayton, Concord, Discovery Bay, Knightsen, Martinez, Oakley, Pacheco, Pittsburg, Pleasant Hill and Walnut Creek.

When you’re riding your bike, you’ll find hazards everywhere: car doors, gravel in the street, dogs crossing your path. Crashes can happen, even to those with awesome biking skills. In a fight between your head and the pavement, the pavement always wins, so protect your skull and the valuable brain matter inside by wearing a properly fitted, certified helmet.

About

Heads Up! is part of Street Smarts Diablo Region, which is made available by the TDM programs of TRANSPAC and TRANSPLAN.
