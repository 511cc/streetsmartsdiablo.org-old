Programs

Grades K-2: Beeps

The star of this 30-minute assembly is Beeps, an animated robotic car that entertains and educates K-2 students about bicycle and pedestrian safety.

Teaching Moments:

  • Traffic signals and street signs
  • Stop, Look, and Listen
  • Wear a helmet
  • School-specific safety messages

Grades 3-5: Heads Up!

Heads Up! is an engaging 30-minute assembly, featuring video clips and demonstrations to teach students the importance of wearing a helmet while riding bikes, skateboards, and scooters.

Teaching Moments:

  • Stop, Look, and Listen
  • Wear a helmet
  • School-specific safety messages

Grades 6-8: Bike Smart

Street Smarts staff visit middle schools on a chosen day to provide free helmets to students who ride bikes, skateboards, and scooters to school. Students are taught proper helmet fit and street-wise skills by local Flatland BMX star Pete Brandt.

Grades 9-12: CHP Teen Driving Program

Street Smarts Diablo has partnered with the California Highway Patrol to bring the “Start Smart” program to high schools. “Start Smart” is a free 2-hour driver safety education class for new and future teen drivers, as well as their parents and guardians.
