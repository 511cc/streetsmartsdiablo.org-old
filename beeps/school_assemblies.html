School Assemblies

Street Smarts Diablo staff will bring Beeps to your school for a 30-minute assembly for grades K-2. The program highlights street-wise education:

  • Look left, look right, and look again
  • Traffic sign and symbol recognition
  • Importance of wearing a helmet
  • Railroad crossing safety awareness
  • Crossing at a crosswalk with/without traffic signal controls

The assembly is held in the school's gym or cafeteria. Beeps workbooks and tattoos are provided to all K-2 students. To reserve a Beeps assembly, contact info@streetsmartdiablo.org.

Street Smarts Diablo delivers the Beeps program at no cost to grade levels K-2 at all public schools in Antioch, Bay Point, Brentwood, Byron, Clayton, Concord, Discovery Bay, Knightsen, Martinez, Oakley, Pacheco, Pittsburg, Pleasant Hill and Walnut Creek.
