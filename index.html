Street Smarts Diablo delivers bicycle and pedestrian safety programs at no cost to every public school in Antioch, Brentwood, Clayton, Concord, Martinez, Oakley, Pittsburg, Pleasant Hill, Walnut Creek and unincorporated areas of central and east Contra Costa County.

About

Street Smarts Diablo is proudly brought to you by the Contra Costa Transportation Authority.
